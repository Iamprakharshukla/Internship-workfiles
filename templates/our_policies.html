Refund Policy – UDAAN Society
UDAAN Society is grateful for your support of our mission to help underserved and neglected communities. Donations made through our website are used to directly fund our programs and services.

Donations are generally non-refundable. However, we understand that mistakes can happen. We will consider a refund in the following limited circumstance:

Fraudulent Transaction: If you believe a fraudulent transaction has been made on your account resulting in a donation to UDAAN Society, please contact us immediately at mail@udaansociety.org. We will investigate the situation and process a refund if we can verify that the donation was unauthorized. Please note: Refunds will only be issued to the original payment method used for the donation.

For any questions regarding our refund policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at mail@udaansociety.org.

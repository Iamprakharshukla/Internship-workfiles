CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

Campus Ambassadors (CA) act as the connection between a company and the university they are studying in. They are the main and direct contact with the company and are the voice of the company on their campus.

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Selected Campus Ambassadors day-to-day responsibilities include:

  1. To enroll Volunteers
  2. To conduct social activities within and around the campus, Who Is A Campus Ambassador?
  3. Campus Ambassadors (CA) act as the connection between a company and the university they are studying in. They are the main and direct contact with the company and are the voice of the company on their campus.
  4. To create social responsibility scenario.

WHO CAN APPLY

Only those candidates can apply who:

  1. Have relevant skills and interests.
  2. Could take some time out for social activities.

SELECTION PROCEDURE

  1. Good Counseling ability.
  2. Command over Local and English language.
  3. Report Writing for Coordination among activities.
